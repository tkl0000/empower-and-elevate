Career Night

July 27th, 2022
7:00-8:15 P.M. (PST)

Event Information

Through a virtual meeting, accomplished career professionals will share their experiences with middle and high school students. We will have breakout rooms for each speaker, and students will be able to choose which room they want to go into. Career Night will provide a wonderful opportunity for students to get advice and learn from professionals in a variety of career fields. Personal interactions with these professionals will help students on their path to identify and pursue their career passions.

The Zoom link will be provided to students and parents after registration.

All students in middle or high school are welcome to join.
