Who are We?

Empower and Elevate is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. We are looking to offer mentorships and internships for high school students who are low-income or of underrepresented backgrounds. Our mentorship program connects students with experienced career professionals. We ask our mentors to connect students to internship opportunities at their workplace. Internships serve as a jumpstart to students' careers, providing students with experience and networks in the workplace. Mentors and mentees are paired based on career interests indicated on our sign-up forms which can be found below. Our mentorship program is free of charge for students.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reach out to low-income and underrepresented high school student communities and provide opportunities to pursue their career interests. We are looking to empower these students with resources to compete in high-tech world. We want our program to have a profound effect on students' lives.
